Accommodation

We take pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally.

Flight reservations

We offer cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made.

Car hire

We are an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. A mutually beneficial relationship exists with Europcar, Budget and Avis from with special rates which are passed onto our customers.

Travel insurance

We provide travel insurance policies provided by AIG, Hollard and TIC, as a 24 hour, 7 day a week emergency service to all customers.

Accounting services

We issue an invoice to the customer on receipt of a customer’s instruction and post the following day. Car hire, tours, hotel, etc., are billed on receipt of the supplier’s invoice.

Visas

We have a department which offers professional and timeous visa services. The department also advises clients regarding visa requirements and/or any other information relevant to a specific destination.
